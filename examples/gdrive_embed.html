Convert the link generated by Google drive from https://drive.google.com/file/d/[image_id]/view?usp=sharing to https://drive.google.com/uc?export=view&id=[image_id] for use in your image tag
Google drive makes it easy to host images online and share it anywhere. If you intend to add an image from your google drive account to you HTML code, it is pretty easy!

Step 1. Upload image to your google drive

First, go to your drive account (obviously, lol). Then you click on the New button and then add the image file (or folder) you wish to save on you Google drive, and tadaa! the image is now on your drive.

Step 2. Get the shareable link

Right click on the image in your google drive and then select share. This will then lead to a modal display, then you select Get sharable link. Copy this link to clipboard.

Step 3. Embed link in your code

A typical link is of this format https://drive.google.com/file/d/[image_id]/view?usp=sharing. Adding this to your img tag will not display the image. The link can be tweaked to https://drive.google.com/uc?export=view&id=[image_id]. This new link can be used on your image tags and image displayed as desired.
